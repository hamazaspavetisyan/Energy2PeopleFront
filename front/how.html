How does it work?

Energy2People allows to the user the visualization of the renewable energy resources of any location in the world and the evaluation of its energy potential.

Map visualization

The user can visualize the available energy resources in the Home tab under the Map option. The data can be requested in yearly, monthly and daily averages that are displayed along time.
The retrieval of the data can be done in 4 simple steps:
  • Time period selection. The possible options are:
      Yearly statistics: Select the period based on years in the Yearly tab
      Monthly statistics: Select the months within a specific year in the Monthly tab. The selected year is defined in the Yearly tab.
      Daily statistics: Select a period based on days in the Daily tab.
  • Select the geophysical parameter for data extraction in the Measure tab
  • Select the statistics to compute in the selected aggregation period. The possible options are:
    • Total
    • Average
    • Maximum
    • Minimum
  • Click on calculate!

Data analysis

The application allows the estimation of energy generation at a given location based on the available renewable energy resources and the proposed facility.
The user can describe the facility based on an intuitive list of parameters:
  • Surface of solar panels
  • Number of wind turbines
  • Quality of the facility
The description of the facility quality is based on available commercial products:

Once the facility has been described, the user selects where to place it either using an specific address or by manually entering the latitude and longitude coordinates.
Finally, press on calculate to get the results!
The provided results are:
  • The daily average generated energy
  • The number of homes to be powered with that energy
  • The equivalent value of the generated energy
  • The facility cost
  • Time to recover the invesment
